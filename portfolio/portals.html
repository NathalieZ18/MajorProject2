Who Are They?







Due to the fact that Cry Baby is an empath, she does not perish but rather reincarnates.
The passage of time causes her bodies to deteriorate, but her soul is unaffected, save for the fact that it retains memories of a prior incarnation.
She grows as a result of the experiences that are depicted in Melanie's songs, official music videos, storybooks, concert tours, and other forms of artistic expression.
She is, in essence, a transcendental creature that possesses infinite power and is traveling on an everlasting trip.











Family



Cry Baby's family consisted of her older brother, mother, and father. Her father's extramarital affair was the root cause of her mother's depression and alcoholism.
Since Cry Baby's family always put on a performance whenever they were in the company of outsiders,
she began to think that everything she saw was an act. ("Dollhouse"). When her mother discovered her husband was having an affair, she took her own life.
This severely weakened an already dysfunctional family. ("Sippy Cup").
While at school, Cry Baby couldn't reach her mother since she had been drinking and passed asleep. (K-12).









Appearance and Evolution







Cry Baby has brown eyes (which can turn entirely black at times) and a gap between her two front teeth;
her hair is worn in two twin-drill pigtails with bows of different colors throughout the novel. She wears a dress, shoes,
and pastel yellow socks most of the time, similar to the other characters. In performances videos, her hair is dyed
on one side and black on the other. The music videos and scenarios all have their own unique colors and design.

Cry Baby's K-12 uniform is a customized version of the one worn by students at the K-12 Sleepaway School.
She has changed the color of the pink frock to purple and woven flowers into the sleeves. In addition,
she dons a clothes in the manner of the Victorian era when performing in the school play. Later on,
she dresses in a swimsuit and a dress with a cake skirt. The colored side of her hair is typically blonde,
however it may occasionally be seen to change colors into coral, purple, and orange.
Cry Baby, much like Melanie, also has a gap between her front teeth.

In PORTALS, Cry Baby is seen to have been reincarnated as a divine entity that originates from a different realm.
Her complexion is pink, both of her eyes are blue, her lips are a dark purple, and she has
green hair that comes in a variety of colors. She can be seen wearing a variety of garments that are influenced by nature,
such as the mushroom shoes she wears in the music video for "DEATH" or the vegetation-like curtains she wears over herself
during her performance at Lollapalooza. Such an example can be seen in the image above.
Soon after her rebirth, she is able to fly with the aid of a set of wings.
